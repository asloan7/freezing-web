Least Daily Variance

This prize is dedicated in memory of Chris Maimone (CPTJohnC on the forum).

This prize is given to the player with the lowest avarage variance in miles per day. To qualify you must ride at least 50 days (about 2/3 of the total days in BAFS) and average more than 2 miles per ride day. If your name is in red, you don't qualify.

Further details in the forum post.

Sponsored by jrenaut.
