Food Rescue Rides

BikeArlington and Food Rescue DC are partnering on a pointless prize to encourage Freezing Saddlers to get some miles while doing some good. Food Rescue is a nonprofit coordinates volunteers who pick-up excess healthy food from local food donors and deliver it directly to local social service agencies that feed the food insecure.

Sign up on the Food Rescue DC website. Make food rescue rides, tag them with #foodrescue. If you make multiple rescues on one ride, append the number of rescues to the tag with an x like so: #foodrescuex2.

Prize sponsored by henry.
